Pilau

Description

Pilau is a fragrant Kenyan rice dish cooked with spices, meat, and broth.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • Meat (beef or chicken)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Pilau masala
  • Water or broth

Steps

  1. Fry onions, garlic, and spices.
  2. Add meat and cook.
  3. Add rice and mix well.
  4. Add water and simmer.
  5. Cook until rice is ready.
