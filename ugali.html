Ugali

Description

Ugali is a staple Kenyan dish made from maize flour and water. It is simple and served with vegetables or meat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 3 cups water

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add maize flour slowly while stirring.
  3. Keep stirring until thick.
  4. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
