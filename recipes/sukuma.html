Sukuma Wiki

Description

Sukuma wiki is a popular vegetable dish in Kenya made using collard greens, onions, and tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Kale (sukuma wiki)
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil and fry onions.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook.
  3. Add chopped sukuma wiki.
  4. Cook until soft.
  5. Serve hot.
